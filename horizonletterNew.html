Thank you and welcome to the 5th Annual Sustainability Summit! Sustainability is defined as “the ability to exist constantly.” In the 21st century, this has come to represent the dream that the human race continues to advance technologically, economically, socially, etc. without exploiting the Earth’s resources and causing potential future harm. Today more than ever, sustainability goals are of the utmost importance for businesses and individuals alike as we usher in a new era of the modern world. Right now, the rapid decline of the natural world, our natural resources as well as the climate change crisis are culminating in irreversible damage to our planet, unless we take immediate action.


Our focus at the Sustainability Summit 2021 is to ensure we are equipped with the tools to meet the needs of the present without compromising future generations. Check out the Event Schedule for Workshops on topics like Climatetech Start Up Lightning Pitches, Financing Climate Solutions and What's Next for Climate Policy. The schedule is jam packed with amazing content, but if you double-book don’t worry, you can catch up on sessions you missed in our Video On Demand library. Explore our Expo Hall and learn about how our exhibitors are fighting for the Earth every day. Join a Networking Happy Hour with fellow climate-warrior attendees and discuss best practices that have worked for you.


We hope to inspire you throughout this conference but also have you leave here with tangible actions you can apply to your home, work and other environments. It all starts today, with you!
